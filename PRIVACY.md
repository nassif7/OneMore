# Privacy Policy — OneMore

_Last updated: April 8, 2026_

## Overview

OneMore is built with privacy in mind. We do not collect, store, or share any personal data.

## Data Collection

OneMore collects no personal information. All data you enter in the app (cigarette logs, timestamps) is stored exclusively on your device using local storage. It never leaves your device.

## Third-Party Services

OneMore does not use any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

## Notifications

OneMore uses local notifications scheduled on your device. These are not push notifications from a server — no data is sent anywhere.

## Children's Privacy

OneMore does not collect data from anyone, including children under 13.

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted at [yoursite.com/privacy].
